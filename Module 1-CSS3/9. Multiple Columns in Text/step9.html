Community Bulletin

This week in Gokuldham Society was nothing short of spectacular! On Monday, Popatlal surprised everyone by announcing his latest matchmaking horoscope reading — spoiler alert: still no good news. Meanwhile, Jethalal’s TV showroom is finally up and running again after that epic power outage. Thanks to Sodhi's jugaad skills, electricity was restored in record time!

Wednesday’s rainstorm brought an impromptu antakshari night in the clubhouse. From Iyer's unbeatable old classics to Daya Bhabhi’s energetic "garba style" songs, the evening was a melody of madness and memories. Tarak Mehta, as usual, played peacekeeper during the soft clash between Jethalal and Babita Ji about "volume settings".

Upcoming Events: Don’t miss this Sunday’s *Seva Diwas*, where residents will join hands for a neighborhood clean-up. Later in the evening, there will be a special screening of "Sholay" on the clubhouse projector followed by free soda and samosas courtesy of Abdul!

In health news, Dr. Haathi recommends everyone to increase their immunity with homemade kadha — recipe available on the society notice board. Tapu Sena, meanwhile, has announced a secret mission to stop Bhide from enforcing early night curfews — more updates soon!
